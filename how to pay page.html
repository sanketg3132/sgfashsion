About Pay on Delivery



Pay on Delivery' (POD) includes Cash on Delivery (COD) as well as additional digital payment facilities at your doorstep. You can pay for 'Pay on Delivery' orders by cash at all locations and by Debit card / Credit card in select locations.

Pay on Delivery is available as a payment method for all items that are Fulfilled by Amazon, Prime Eligible and some seller fulfilled items. Pay on Delivery orders can be paid using Cash or Credit card/Debit card. Please check with the delivery associate if you can pay by using Sgfashions Pay link and card swipe machine/mPOS (subject to availability of payment instrument at your location). We accept Visa, MasterCard, Maestro and Rupay debit and credit cards from all banks.

How to pay using card swipe machine/mPOS -
1.Ask your delivery agent for a card swipe machine/mPOS
2.Swipe/insert your card
3.Enter your card ATM PIN number to complete the payment
4.Post successful payment, you will get a confirmation message.


How to pay using SGfashsion Pay link –
1.Ask your delivery agent for Pay link SMS on your smartphone which has internet connectivity
2.Click on the link in SMS and initiate payment
3.Select your payment method and complete payment using your credentials
4.Post successful payment, you will get a confirmation message.


Pay on Delivery cannot be used in conjunction with the Amazon Pay balance as a payment mode. If you deselect Amazon Pay balance as a payment mode on the Amazon.in payment page, Pay on Delivery may become available as a payment mode, provided the item and delivery pin code are eligible. We only collect the amount printed on the invoice.
